Product Overview

Riverhead's Premium Black Beans are a staple in kitchens worldwide, known for their versatility and nutritional value. Sourced with care, these beans maintain their shape well during cooking, making them ideal for hearty soups, vibrant salads, classic rice and beans, and flavorful dips like black bean salsa or hummus.

Their deep, rich flavor profile complements a wide array of spices and ingredients. Whether you're preparing a traditional Cuban Frijoles Negros, a Southwestern chili, or a modern vegetarian burger, our black beans provide a satisfying and wholesome base. They are cleaned and packed to ensure you receive only the highest quality product, ready for your culinary creations.

Origin: Primarily USA and Canada.

Taste Profile: Mildly sweet, earthy, with a creamy texture when cooked.

Basic Cooking Instructions
  1. Rinse beans thoroughly under cold water.
  2. Soak beans: For every 1 cup of dry beans, use 3-4 cups of water. Soak overnight (8-12 hours) or use the quick soak method (boil for 2-3 minutes, then let stand for 1 hour).
  3. Drain soaking water and rinse beans again.
  4. Place beans in a large pot with fresh water to cover (about 2 inches above beans).
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours, or until tender. Cooking time may vary.
  6. Add salt and seasonings during the last 30 minutes of cooking if desired.

Tip: Do not add acidic ingredients like tomatoes or vinegar until beans are tender, as they can slow down the cooking process.
